<p><p>The PE blow molded containers market primarily includes two types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE is known for its strength, resistance to impact, and chemical durability, making it ideal for packaging milk, detergents, and industrial products. LDPE, being more flexible and transparent, is typically used for containers that require lightweight and easy-to-form characteristics, such as squeezable bottles and food packaging. Both materials offer diverse applications across industries due to their efficiency and recyclability.</p></p>
