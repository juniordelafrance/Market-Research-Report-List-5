<p><p>The specialty grade carbon black market comprises various types including Furnace Black, produced through oil or gas combustion and known for its consistency; Channel Black, derived from the incomplete combustion of oils, offering higher purity; Thermal Black, created by thermal decomposition, known for its low structure; Acetylene Black, made from the pyrolysis of acetylene, characterized by high conductivity; and Others, which includes niche variants. Each type serves specific applications in industries like rubber, plastics, and electronics, depending on their unique properties.</p></p>
