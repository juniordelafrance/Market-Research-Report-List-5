<p><p>Signals Intelligence (SIGINT) encompasses various market types, primarily Electronic Intelligence (ELINT) and Communications Intelligence (COMINT). ELINT involves the analysis of non-communication electronic signals, primarily from radar systems, to gather information about weapon systems and military capabilities. COMINT focuses on intercepting and analyzing communication signals, including voice and data transmissions, to gain insights into adversary intentions and strategies. Other SIGINT categories may include cyber intelligence and measurement and signature intelligence (MASINT), which further enhance situational awareness in national security contexts.</p></p>
