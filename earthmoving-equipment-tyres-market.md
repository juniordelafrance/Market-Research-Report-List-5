<p><p>The Earthmoving Equipment Tyres market features two primary types: radial tyres and bias tyres. Radial tyres have a construction where the internal cords run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, durability, and heat dissipation, making them suitable for long-distance and varied terrain usage. In contrast, bias tyres have cords laid at an angle, providing enhanced stability and puncture resistance, making them ideal for rugged applications and short hauls. Each type caters to specific operational needs in construction and mining sectors.</p></p>
