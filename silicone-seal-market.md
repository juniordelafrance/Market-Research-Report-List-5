<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive</li><li>Radiation Curing</li><li>Room Temperature Vulcanizing</li><li>Thermoset</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone seal market encompasses various types, each catering to specific applications. Pressure-sensitive silicone seals provide strong adhesion and are used in automotive and construction industries. Radiation curing silicone seals offer rapid curing and durability, making them ideal for electronic components. Room temperature vulcanizing silicones cure at ambient temperatures, suitable for general-purpose sealing in household and industrial applications. Thermoset silicones are heat-resistant and provide excellent stability, commonly used in demanding environments such as aerospace and automotive sectors.</p></p>
