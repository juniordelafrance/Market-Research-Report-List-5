<p><p>The lithium battery anode graphitization market encompasses two primary methods: direct and indirect. The direct method involves the thermal treatment of carbon-based precursors at high temperatures to produce graphite anodes, optimizing capacity and efficiency. In contrast, the indirect method entails initial conversion of the precursor into an intermediate form before subsequent graphitization, allowing for tailored characteristics and enhanced performance. Both approaches are crucial for advancing lithium battery technology, catering to different performance needs and manufacturing efficiencies.</p></p>
