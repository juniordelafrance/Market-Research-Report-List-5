<p><p>The Boron Trifluoride market is primarily categorized into two methods of production: the Sulfuric Acid Method and the Thermal Decomposition Method. The Sulfuric Acid Method involves the reaction of boron oxide with sulfuric acid, yielding boron trifluoride and water. Meanwhile, the Thermal Decomposition Method entails heating boron trichloride in the presence of hydrogen fluoride to produce boron trifluoride. Each method influences production costs, purity levels, and environmental impacts, thereby affecting market dynamics and demand for boron trifluoride in various applications.</p></p>
