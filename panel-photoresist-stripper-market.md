<p><ul><li>Solvent</li><li>Semi-aqueous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The panel photoresist stripper market is categorized into two main types: solvent-based and semi-aqueous strippers. Solvent-based strippers utilize organic solvents to effectively dissolve photoresist materials, offering rapid processing and high efficiency but may present environmental concerns. In contrast, semi-aqueous strippers blend solvents with water, providing a more environmentally friendly option. They maintain effective photoresist removal capabilities while reducing harmful emissions, appealing to industries focused on sustainability while still achieving reliable performance in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
