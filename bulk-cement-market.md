<p><p>The bulk cement market comprises various types catering to specific construction needs. Portland cement is the most widely used, known for its durability and strength. White cement is used for aesthetic applications due to its color and is often found in architectural designs. Hydraulic cement gains strength when mixed with water, making it ideal for damp environments. Alumina cement, which sets rapidly and withstands high temperatures, is used in specialized construction applications. Each type plays a unique role in construction processes.</p></p>
