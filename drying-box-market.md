<p><p>The drying box market features several types, including the electrothermal drying box and vacuum drying box. Electrothermal drying boxes utilize electric heating elements to promote rapid drying, offering precise temperature control for various applications. In contrast, vacuum drying boxes operate under reduced pressure, which lowers boiling points and facilitates gentle drying of heat-sensitive materials, preserving their quality. Both types cater to distinct industrial needs, from laboratories to manufacturing, highlighting their versatility in moisture removal processes.</p></p>
