<p><p>The vacuum disc filters market is segmented into two main types: vertical and horizontal vacuum disc filters. Vertical vacuum disc filters feature discs positioned vertically, allowing for efficient filtration and cake discharge, making them ideal for applications with limited space. Conversely, horizontal vacuum disc filters are designed with horizontally aligned discs, enhancing accessibility for maintenance and providing a larger surface area for filtration. Both types are widely used in industries such as mining, chemical processing, and wastewater treatment for effective solid-liquid separation.</p></p>
