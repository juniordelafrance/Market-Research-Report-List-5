<p><p>The AR head-mounted display (HMD) optical waveguide market features three main types: </p><p>1. **Geometric Waveguide**: Utilizes refraction to direct light, allowing for a compact and lightweight design, suitable for various applications. </p><p>2. **Diffractive Waveguide (Surface Relief Grating)**: Employs surface patterns to manipulate light paths, providing high-resolution imagery and efficient light management, often in thinner form factors. </p><p>3. **Diffractive Waveguide (Volume Holographic Gratings)**: Utilizes holographic elements for advanced light manipulation, offering superior image quality and broad viewing angles while maximizing light throughput.</p></p>
