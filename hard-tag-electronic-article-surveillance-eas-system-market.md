<p><p>Hard Tag Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) systems utilize various technologies to prevent theft in retail environments. Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) systems use tags that emit signals to track items. Acoustic Magnetic (AM) systems employ magnetic fields to detect tagged items. Radio Frequency (RF) systems use radio waves to identify and deactivate tags at exits. Electromagnetic Wave (EM) systems operate through magnetic fields and require specific tags for detection.
