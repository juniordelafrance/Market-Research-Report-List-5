<p><p>The synthetic dye and pigment market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Acidic Dyes** are water-soluble and commonly used on wool and nylon.</p><p>2. **Basic Dyes** are cationic and suitable for acrylic and some polyester fibers. </p><p>3. **Direct Dyes** apply directly to cellulose fibers using an alkaline solution. </p><p>4. **Reactive Dyes** form covalent bonds with fibers, ensuring robust wash-fastness. </p><p>5. **Solvent Dyes** dissolve in organic solvents, primarily used for plastics and varnishes. </p><p>6. **Disperse Dyes** are used for synthetic fibers like polyester. </p><p>7. **Others** include specialized applications in various industries.</p></p>
