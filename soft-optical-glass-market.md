<p><ul><li>Single Coated Glass</li><li>Double Coated Glass</li><li>Triple Coated Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soft optical glass market is categorized into three types based on coating: single coated, double coated, and triple coated glass. Single coated glass features one layer that enhances light transmission while minimizing reflections. Double coated glass has two layers, providing improved anti-reflective properties and enhanced durability. Triple coated glass incorporates three layers for superior light performance, reduced glare, and better scratch resistance. Each type offers varying levels of optical clarity and protection, catering to different applications in photography, optics, and display technologies.</p></p>
