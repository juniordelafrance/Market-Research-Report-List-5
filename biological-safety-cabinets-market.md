<p><p>Biological Safety Cabinets (BSCs) are classified into three main types based on their design and protection levels. Class I cabinets provide personnel and environmental protection by filtering air before it's released outside. Class II cabinets, the most common, offer aster and product protection through HEPA filtration, creating a sterile work environment with a front airflow barrier. Class III cabinets provide the highest level of containment, designed for handling infectious agents, featuring airtight construction and glove ports for maximum safety. Each category serves specific laboratory needs.</p></p>
