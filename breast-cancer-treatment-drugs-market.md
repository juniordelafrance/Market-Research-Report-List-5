<p><p>The breast cancer treatment drugs market encompasses several key types. HER2 inhibitors target the HER2 protein to slow cancer growth, often used in HER2-positive cases. Mitotic inhibitors disrupt the cell division process, effectively halting tumor proliferation. Anti-metabolites interfere with DNA and RNA synthesis, limiting cancer cell reproduction. Aromatase inhibitors block estrogen production, crucial for hormone-receptor-positive breast cancers. Each category plays a vital role in personalized treatment strategies, enhancing patient outcomes in breast cancer management.</p></p>
