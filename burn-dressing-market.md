<p><p>Burn dressings are categorized into two main types: water-resistant and normal. </p><p>Water-resistant burn dressings provide a protective barrier against moisture, allowing patients to shower or engage in activities without compromising the dressing's integrity. This type is ideal for individuals requiring prolonged use in moist environments. </p><p>Normal burn dressings, on the other hand, are typically designed for standard protection of burns and abrasions, focusing on absorption and comfort without specific water-resistant capabilities. Both types play vital roles in promoting healing and preventing infection.</p></p>
