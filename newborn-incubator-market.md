<p><p>The newborn incubator market is divided into two key types: transport baby incubators and normal baby incubators. Transport baby incubators are portable devices designed for safe, regulated environments during medical emergencies or transfers, ensuring the infant's stability and care on the move. Normal baby incubators, on the other hand, are fixed units found in neonatal intensive care units (NICUs) that provide a controlled environment to support premature or ill newborns, promoting development and recovery in a stable, monitored setting.</p></p>
