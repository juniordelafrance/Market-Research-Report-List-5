<p><p>The die-attach materials market is primarily categorized into two types: die attach paste and die attach wire. Die attach paste consists of adhesive materials used to bond semiconductor chips to substrates, providing thermal and electrical conductivity. It is essential for modern electronics to ensure reliability and performance. Die attach wire, on the other hand, involves thin wires used to connect the die to the package, enabling electrical signals to pass effectively. Both types are critical for manufacturing advanced electronic components.</p></p>
