<p><ul><li>Aboveground Installation</li><li>Tunnel Installation</li><li>Vertical Installation</li><li>Direct Burial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas Insulated Transmission Lines (GITLs) are categorized based on their installation methods. Aboveground installations utilize towers or structures to support the lines, allowing visibility and easier maintenance. Tunnel installations involve laying GITLs in underground tunnels, protecting them from environmental factors while enhancing urban aesthetics. Vertical installations maximize space in constrained areas, using vertical shafts for routing. Direct burial involves embedding GITLs directly into the ground, offering a discreet solution, particularly effective in densely populated or environmentally sensitive regions.</p></p>
