<p><p>The Car Vacuum Pumps market includes **Electric Vacuum Pumps** and **Mechanical Vacuum Pumps**. Electric vacuum pumps are powered by the vehicle's electrical system, offering precise control and efficiency, often used in modern vehicles with advanced braking systems. Mechanical vacuum pumps are driven by the engine, typically through a belt or camshaft, and are common in older or diesel vehicles. Both types generate vacuum pressure for systems like brakes, emissions control, and HVAC, catering to different vehicle designs and performance needs.</p></p>
