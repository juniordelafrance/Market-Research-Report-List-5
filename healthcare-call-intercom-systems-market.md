Mobile type systems allow healthcare staff to communicate through smartphones or tablets, enhancing mobility and response times. Landline type systems utilize traditional wired telecommunication networks, ensuring reliable communication within fixed locations. Each type serves distinct needs in enhancing patient care and operational efficiency in healthcare settings.</p></p>
