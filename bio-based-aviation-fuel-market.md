<p><ul><li>HEFA-SPK</li><li>FT-SPK</li><li>ATJ-SPK</li><li>Co-processing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bio-based aviation fuel market comprises several types, including HEFA-SPK (Hydroprocessed Esters and Fatty Acids Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene), which is derived from vegetable oils and animal fats. FT-SPK (Fischer-Tropsch Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) is produced from biomass through gasification. ATJ-SPK (Alcohol-to-Jet Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) utilizes alcohols as feedstock. Co-processing involves blending biofuels with conventional jet fuel during refining. Other methods include alternative pathways like pyrolysis and biochemical processes, expanding the portfolio of sustainable aviation fuel options.</p></p>
