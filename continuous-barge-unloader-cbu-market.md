<p><strong>The Continuous Barge Unloader (CBU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bridge Type</li><li>Cantilever Type</li><li>Elevating Type</li><li>Traveling Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Barge Unloaders (CBUs) are critical for efficient bulk handling in ports and terminals. The market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Bridge Type**: Features a fixed bridge structure, allowing cargo transfer from barges to conveyor systems.</p><p>2. **Cantilever Type**: Extends over the barge, providing flexibility in unloading without needing significant infrastructure.</p><p>3. **Elevating Type**: Combines lifting and conveying, ideal for unloading at varying heights.</p><p>4. **Traveling Type**: Moves along the barge, optimizing reach and efficiency for large cargo operations.</p></p>
