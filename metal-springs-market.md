<p><p>The metal springs market comprises various types, primarily helical and leaf springs. Helical springs are coiled steel structures used to absorb shock, providing flexibility and strength, commonly found in automotive and machinery applications. Leaf springs consist of multiple layers of thin metal strips, offering robust support and load distribution, typically used in vehicles like trucks and trailers for suspension systems. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing performance, durability, and comfort in various engineering and transportation sectors.</p></p>
