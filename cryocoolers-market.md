<p><strong>The Cryocoolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gifford-Mcmahon Cryocoolers</li><li>Pulse-Tube Cryocoolers</li><li>Stirling Cryocoolers</li><li>Joule-Thomson Cryocoolers</li><li>Brayton Cryocoolers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryocoolers market consists of various types, each with distinct operational mechanisms. Gifford-McMahon cryocoolers utilize a gaseous refrigerant to achieve low temperatures through expansion and compression cycles. Pulse-Tube cryocoolers employ pressure oscillations to cool without moving parts, enhancing reliability. Stirling cryocoolers operate on a closed regenerative cycle, providing efficient cooling. Joule-Thomson cryocoolers utilize gas expansion to achieve low temperatures, while Brayton cryocoolers leverage a gas compression cycle. Each type caters to different applications, from industrial to medical and aerospace.</p></p>
