<p><p>Pressure sustaining valves are essential components in fluid control systems, designed to maintain a specified upstream pressure within a pipeline or system. <p><p>Pressure sustaining valves market can be categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their orientation and installation preferences. Vertical pressure sustaining valves are designed to be installed vertically, optimizing space in constrained environments and often found in high-rise applications. Conversely, horizontal pressure sustaining valves are installed horizontally, making them suitable for pipelines with limited vertical clearance. Both types serve the important function of maintaining system pressure, ensuring consistent flow and operational efficiency across various applications.</p></p>
