<p><p>Ultrasonic level sensors are key devices used for measuring liquid and solid levels in various applications. They are categorized into two main types: continuous ultrasonic level sensors and point ultrasonic level sensors. Continuous ultrasonic level sensors provide real-time measurements of level variations, offering ongoing monitoring, while point ultrasonic level sensors detect levels at a specific point, ideal for applications needing precise thresholds. Both types utilize ultrasonic waves for non-contact measurements, ensuring accuracy and reliability in challenging environments.</p></p>
