Capacitive sensors measure pressure by detecting changes in capacitance, ideal for hygienic applications. Potentiometric sensors determine pressure through voltage variations, providing reliable measurements. Other types may include piezoelectric and optical sensors, expanding the market with diverse technologies tailored for ensuring safety and quality in food and beverage processing.</p></p>
