Digital optical liquid level sensors utilize advanced technology to provide precise and discrete level readings, often featuring digital displays and integration capabilities with automated systems. In contrast, analog optical liquid level sensors offer continuous level measurements and are typically simpler in design, providing real-time readings through varying signal outputs. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, ensuring efficient monitoring and management of liquid levels.</p></p>
