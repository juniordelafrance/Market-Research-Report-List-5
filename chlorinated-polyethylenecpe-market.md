<p><p>Chlorinated Polyethylene (CPE) is a versatile polymer derived from polyethylene that is enhanced through chlorination. The market for CPE is primarily divided into two types: thermoplastic resin type and elastomer rubber type. The thermoplastic resin type is characterized by its rigidity and is often used in applications requiring structural integrity, such as coatings and adhesives. In contrast, the elastomer rubber type exhibits flexibility and durability, making it suitable for applications like seals, gaskets, and flexible tubing. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
