<p><p>The automotive glass market for windshields primarily consists of three types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for strength and shatters into small, blunt pieces upon impact, enhancing safety. Laminated glass consists of two glass layers with a plastic interlayer, providing better sound insulation and protection against shattering. "Others" includes specialty glasses with unique coatings or technologies aimed at specific functionalities, such as tinted or heated windshields, catering to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
