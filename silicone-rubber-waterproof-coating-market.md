<p><p>The silicone rubber waterproof coating market is primarily divided into two types: liquid silicone rubber waterproof coating and dry silicone rubber waterproof coating. Liquid silicone rubber coatings are flexible and easy to apply, forming a strong, waterproof barrier that adheres well to various surfaces. In contrast, dry silicone rubber coatings provide a solid, cured layer that offers enhanced durability and chemical resistance. Both types are used in diverse applications, including construction, automotive, and electronics, to ensure moisture protection and longevity.</p></p>
