<p><p>Phenolic resins for casting are thermosetting polymers known for their excellent mechanical properties and heat resistance. The market is primarily divided into two types: granular resin and flaky resin. Granular resin features small, rounded particles that facilitate easy handling and mixing, making it suitable for various casting applications. In contrast, flaky resin has a flat, thin structure, enhancing surface finish and flow characteristics during casting processes. Both types are crucial for industries requiring durable, high-performance materials.</p></p>
