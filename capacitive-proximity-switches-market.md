<p><p>The capacitive proximity switches market is divided into two main types: DC type and AC type. DC type switches operate on low voltage direct current, ideal for applications requiring accurate sensing in automation and control. They offer fast response times and are often used in battery-powered devices. On the other hand, AC type switches work with alternating current and are typically used in industrial environments for detecting various materials, including liquids and solids, due to their robust performance across different conditions.</p></p>
