<p><strong>The Van Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The van wheel speed sensor market is primarily segmented into Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel speed, offering high accuracy and reliability, making them widely favored in modern vehicles. Conversely, Magnetic Electric sensors rely on the generation of voltage from the movement of a magnetic field, typically providing a cost-effective solution. Both types are critical for functions such as anti-lock braking systems (ABS) and traction control, enhancing vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
