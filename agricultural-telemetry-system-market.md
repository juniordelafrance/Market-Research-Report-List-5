In contrast, Multi-Channel Telemetry allows simultaneous monitoring of multiple variables, enabling comprehensive data collection and analysis for larger agricultural setups. This versatility enhances decision-making, optimizing resource management and crop yield while fulfilling the demands of modern precision agriculture practices.</p></p>
