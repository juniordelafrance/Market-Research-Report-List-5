<p><p>The Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) is an electronic system designed to monitor the air pressure inside pneumatic tires on vehicles. TPMS can be either direct, using sensors inside the tires, or indirect, relying on wheel speed sensors.</p><p>The TPMS market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing vehicle safety regulations, rising consumer awareness about road safety, and the growing adoption of advanced automotive technologies. The Asia-Pacific region is emerging as a major growth hub due to rapid urbanization, increasing vehicle production, and rising disposable incomes. <p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) market includes two main types: Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. Direct TPMS uses sensors inside each tire to measure real-time pressure and transmit data to the vehicle's system, offering precise readings. Indirect TPMS relies on wheel speed sensors and ABS to estimate pressure based on rotational differences, making it cost-effective but less accurate. Both systems enhance safety, fuel efficiency, and tire longevity, catering to different consumer needs and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
