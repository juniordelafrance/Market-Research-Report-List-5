<p><ul><li>Non-compacted Strand Steel Wire Rope</li><li>Compacted Strand Steel Wire Rope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anchor wire rope market primarily features two types: non-compacted strand steel wire rope and compacted strand steel wire rope. Non-compacted strand wire rope consists of multiple strands laid together without additional compaction, providing flexibility and ease of handling, making it suitable for various applications. In contrast, compacted strand wire rope undergoes a compaction process, increasing its density and reducing fatigue, which enhances strength and durability. This makes compacted wire rope preferred in demanding environments where enhanced performance is essential.</p></p>
