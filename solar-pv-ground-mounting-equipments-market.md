<p><p>The Solar PV ground mounting equipment market includes primarily two types: crystalline silicon systems and thin film systems. Crystalline silicon systems, known for their high efficiency and durability, are widely used in residential and commercial installations. They consist of rigid panels that maximize sunlight absorption. In contrast, thin film systems are lightweight and flexible, allowing for versatile applications, including unconventional surfaces. While they generally have lower efficiency, their cost-effectiveness and adaptability make them appealing for specific installations, particularly in large-scale projects.</p></p>
