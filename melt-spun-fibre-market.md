<p><p>The melt-spun fiber market is divided into two main types: filament melt-spun fibers and staple melt-spun fibers. Filament melt-spun fibers are continuous strands used in applications like textiles and industrial fabrics, offering high strength and durability. In contrast, staple melt-spun fibers are short, discrete lengths that are often used in spun yarns for textile manufacturing, providing versatility and ease in blending with other fibers. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing product functionality and performance.</p></p>
