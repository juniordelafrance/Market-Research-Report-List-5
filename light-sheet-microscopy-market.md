<p><p>Light sheet microscopy encompasses various techniques such as Selective Plane Illumination Microscopy (SPIM) and Dual-View Selective Plane Illumination Microscopy (DiSPIM). SPIM uses a single light sheet to illuminate the specimen, enabling rapid imaging with minimal photodamage. DiSPIM enhances this by employing two orthogonal light sheets, allowing for improved 3D imaging and reduced background noise. Other emerging techniques in this market may include variations and advancements that enhance resolution, speed, and compatibility with various specimen types.</p></p>
