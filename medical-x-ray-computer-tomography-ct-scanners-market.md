<p><p>Medical X-Ray Computer Tomography (CT) scanners are categorized into three main types based on detector configuration. Single-detector CT uses one detector to capture images, suitable for basic imaging needs. Double-detector CT improves speed and image quality, making it ideal for rapidly assessing patient conditions. Multidetector CT employs multiple detectors, enabling high-resolution images and faster scans, which enhances diagnostic capabilities across various medical fields. Each type serves specific clinical requirements, balancing cost, efficiency, and imaging quality.</p></p>
