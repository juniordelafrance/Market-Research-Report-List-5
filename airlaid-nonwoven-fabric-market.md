<p><p>The airlaid nonwoven fabric market is characterized by three primary bonding types: Latex-bonded, Thermal-bonded, and Multi-bonded. Latex-bonded type uses latex adhesives for improved strength and flexibility, making it suitable for hygiene products. Thermal-bonded type employs heat to fuse fibers, offering lightweight and soft materials ideal for medical and filtration applications. Multi-bonded type combines various bonding methods, ensuring enhanced durability and versatility, catering to a broader range of uses, including automotive and construction industries.</p></p>
