<p><p>The Synthetic Dimethicone market is categorized into three main types based on viscosity levels: low, medium, and high viscosity dimethicone. Low viscosity dimethicone offers a lightweight texture, making it ideal for personal care formulations and hair products. Medium viscosity dimethicone provides a balanced feel, suitable for creams and lotions with better spreadability. High viscosity dimethicone, with its thicker consistency, is preferred in formulations requiring enhanced moisture retention and stability, often used in heavy creams and specialized treatments.</p></p>
