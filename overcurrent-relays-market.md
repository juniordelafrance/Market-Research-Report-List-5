<p><p>The Overcurrent Relays market is categorized into two main types: unit type protection and non-unit type protection. Unit type protection relays are designed to monitor and isolate specific equipment, providing targeted protection by acting only on fault conditions detected within the designated unit. In contrast, non-unit type protection relays offer broader surveillance over the electrical system, protecting multiple components without isolating specific equipment. Both types are essential for ensuring electrical system stability and preventing damage from overloads and short circuits.</p></p>
