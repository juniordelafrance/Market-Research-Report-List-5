<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Humidifier</li><li>Evaporative Humidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mist-free humidifier market primarily includes two types: ultrasonic humidifiers and evaporative humidifiers. Ultrasonic humidifiers use high-frequency vibrations to produce a fine mist without heating water, resulting in energy efficiency and quieter operation. In contrast, evaporative humidifiers rely on a fan to blow air through a wet wick or filter, naturally adding moisture into the air while also purifying it. Both types cater to different preferences and settings, providing effective humidity control without creating excess mist.</p></p>
