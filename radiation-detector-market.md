<p><p>The radiation detector market comprises three main types: gas ionization detectors, semiconductor detectors, and scintillation detectors. Gas ionization detectors utilize gases to identify radiation through ionization processes. Semiconductor detectors, made from materials like silicon, offer high precision and energy resolution, making them suitable for various applications. Scintillation detectors use scintillating materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, enabling efficient detection. Each type serves distinct applications in fields such as healthcare, nuclear energy, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
