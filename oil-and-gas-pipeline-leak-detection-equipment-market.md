<p><p>The oil and gas pipeline leak detection equipment market features several key technologies. Flow meters measure changes in fluid flow, helping identify abnormalities that may indicate leaks. Infra-red detection systems utilize thermal imaging to spot temperature variations caused by leakage, allowing for swift response. Fiber optic sensors use light transmission along fibers to detect changes in pressure, temperature, or strain, providing continuous monitoring. Together, these technologies enhance safety and efficiency in pipeline operations, mitigating environmental risks associated with leaks.</p></p>
