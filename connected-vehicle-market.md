<p><p>The Connected Vehicle Market comprises three main types: Embedded Systems, Tethered Systems, and Integrated Systems. Embedded Systems feature built-in connectivity hardware for real-time data exchange without dependence on external devices. Tethered Systems rely on a mobile device link for connectivity, enhancing features through smartphone integration. Integrated Systems combine both embedded and tethered elements, providing seamless connectivity options and comprehensive on-board systems. Together, these types enhance vehicle functionality, safety, and user experience in the growing connected automotive landscape.</p></p>
