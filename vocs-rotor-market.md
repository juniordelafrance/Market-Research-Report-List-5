<p><p>The VOC rotor market is primarily divided into two types: zeolite VOC rotors and activated carbon VOC rotors. Zeolite VOC rotors utilize zeolite material to adsorb volatile organic compounds due to their high selectivity and thermal stability, making them ideal for environments with varying humidity. Activated carbon VOC rotors, on the other hand, leverage activated carbon's high adsorption capacity across a broad range of VOCs, effectively removing pollutants in industrial and commercial applications. Both types enhance air quality and sustainability.</p></p>
