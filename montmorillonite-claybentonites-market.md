<p><p>Montmorillonite clay, commonly known as bentonite, has distinct market types based on its ionic composition: sodium, calcium, and potassium bentonite. Sodium bentonite has high swelling properties, making it suitable for drilling and sealing applications. Calcium bentonite offers excellent adsorption characteristics, often used in cosmetic and pharmaceutical products. Potassium bentonite, with lower swelling capabilities, is primarily utilized in agricultural soil amendments. Each type serves specific industrial and commercial needs, contributing to a diverse market ecosystem.</p></p>
