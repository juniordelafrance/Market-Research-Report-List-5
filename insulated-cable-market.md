<p><p>The insulated cable market primarily features two types: XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) insulated cables and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) insulated cables. XLPE cables are known for their high thermal resistance and insulation properties, making them suitable for high-voltage applications and harsh environments. Conversely, PVC insulated cables are favored for their flexibility, lower cost, and ease of installation, making them ideal for various residential and commercial applications. Both types cater to specific industry needs, ensuring safe and efficient electrical transmission.</p></p>
