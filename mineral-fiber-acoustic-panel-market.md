<p><p>The Mineral Fiber Acoustic Panel market comprises various types, each with distinct properties. Glasswool is lightweight and offers excellent thermal insulation. Stonewool and Rockwool are known for their fire resistance and sound absorption qualities, making them ideal for industrial applications. Slagwool, derived from steel production, is similar to Rockwool but more affordable. Glass Filaments provide high tensile strength while maintaining acoustic properties. Ceramic Fibres excel in high-temperature applications, showcasing durability and sound attenuation. Other materials may include specialized composites and synthetic fibers.</p></p>
