<p><p>Fire beam detectors are sophisticated devices used for early fire detection in large spaces. The market for these detectors includes two main types: infrared beam detectors and optical beam detectors. Infrared beam detectors utilize infrared light to sense smoke or heat, offering reliable performance in environments with dust or steam. Optical beam detectors, on the other hand, use visible light and rely on changes in light intensity caused by smoke, making them suitable for open areas. Both types enhance fire safety and prevention measures in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
