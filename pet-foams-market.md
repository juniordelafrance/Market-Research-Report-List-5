<p><strong>The PET Foams Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-density Foam</li><li>High-density Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PET foams are categorized into low-density and high-density types, each serving distinct applications. Low-density foam, typically lighter and more flexible, is used in packaging and insulation, offering good thermal resistance and energy absorption. High-density foam, on the other hand, is denser and more rigid, making it suitable for structural components in industries like aerospace and automotive. Both types benefit from the high strength-to-weight ratio of PET, contributing to their popularity in various engineering and manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
