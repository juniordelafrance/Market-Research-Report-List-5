<p><strong>The Magnesium Caseinate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sprayed Type</li><li>Extruded Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnesium caseinate market can be categorized into two main types: sprayed and extruded. Sprayed magnesium caseinate is produced by atomizing a mixture of casein and magnesium, resulting in a fine powder that maintains protein integrity and offers high solubility. In contrast, extruded magnesium caseinate involves forcing the mixture through an extrusion process, which enhances its texture and digestibility. Both types are utilized in food products, dietary supplements, and functional foods, catering to varying consumer preferences for health benefits and product application.</p></p>
