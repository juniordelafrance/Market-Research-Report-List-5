<p><p>The PVC fiber reinforced hose market includes two primary types: winding wire hoses and crocheted wire hoses. Winding wire hoses are constructed by embedding spiral metal wires within layers of PVC, offering enhanced flexibility and durability, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, crocheted wire hoses utilize a woven pattern of wire reinforcement, providing improved strength and resistance to external factors. Both types are widely used in various industries, including agriculture, construction, and automotive, due to their reliability and versatility.</p></p>
