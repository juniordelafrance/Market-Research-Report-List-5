<p><strong>The Solvent Based Epoxy Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Process</li><li>Indirect Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solvent-based epoxy resin market can be categorized into two primary types: direct and indirect processes. The direct process involves mixing epoxy resin with solvents to create a ready-to-use formulation, suitable for applications like coatings and adhesives. In contrast, the indirect process entails initially producing a resin that later requires solvent dilution, often leading to enhanced properties and performance. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs, influencing factors like curing time, viscosity, and final product characteristics.</p></p>
