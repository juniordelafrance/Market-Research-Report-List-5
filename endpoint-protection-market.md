<p><strong>The Endpoint Protection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anti-virus</li><li>Antispyware/Antimalware</li><li>Firewall</li><li>Endpoint Device Control</li><li>Intrusion Prevention</li><li>Endpoint Application Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Endpoint protection encompasses various market types aimed at securing devices from threats. Anti-virus software detects and eliminates malicious software, while antispyware/antimalware focuses on removing spyware and other harmful applications. Firewalls monitor and control network traffic to prevent unauthorized access. Endpoint device control restricts the use of external devices, reducing the risk of data breaches. Intrusion prevention systems actively block potential attacks, and endpoint application control manages and restricts application usage to mitigate risks associated with unauthorized software.</p></p>
