<p><p>The Medical Atherectomy System market comprises various types, including Targeted Atherectomy, which minimizes damage to surrounding tissues while removing plaque; Orbital Atherectomy, utilizing a rotating eccentric crown to ablate calcified lesions; Photoablative (laser) Atherectomy, employing laser energy to vaporize plaque; and Rotational Atherectomy, which uses a high-speed rotating device to mechanically grind away blockages. Each type offers unique advantages for treating cardiovascular conditions, enabling tailored approaches to effectively restore blood flow and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
