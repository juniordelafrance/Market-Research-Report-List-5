<p><p>The industrial tilt sensors market is categorized into single-axis and dual-axis types. Single-axis tilt sensors measure tilt in one direction, commonly used in applications like machinery leveling and slope detection. Dual-axis tilt sensors, on the other hand, measure tilt in two perpendicular directions, providing more comprehensive data for applications such as robotics, construction equipment, and automotive systems. These sensors enhance operational efficiency and safety by accurately monitoring the orientation and stability of various industrial equipment and structures.</p></p>
