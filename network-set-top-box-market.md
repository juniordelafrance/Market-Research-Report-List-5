<p><p>The Network Set Top Box market is primarily categorized into three types: IPTV, OTT, and other markets. IPTV delivers television content through internet protocol networks, utilizing a dedicated internet connection for a seamless viewing experience. OTT (Over-The-Top) bypasses traditional distribution, allowing content streaming via the internet directly to consumers without a subscription to traditional cable services. Other markets may include hybrid boxes that support both IPTV and OTT, integrating various streaming services and conventional broadcasting to enhance user experience.</p></p>
