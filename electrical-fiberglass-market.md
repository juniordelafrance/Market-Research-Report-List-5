<p><p>The electrical fiberglass market is primarily categorized into fiber yarn and fiber roving types. Fiber yarn is a continuous strand of fiberglass used in applications requiring flexibility and strength, making it suitable for weaving and knitting. In contrast, fiber roving consists of loose strands of fiberglass that allow for easy manipulation, ideal for applications like composite materials and insulation. Both types are essential in manufacturing electrical components, enhancing durability, electrical insulation, and thermal resistance in various industrial applications.</p></p>
