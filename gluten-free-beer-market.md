<p><p>The gluten-free beer market consists of two primary types: dedicated gluten-free beers and gluten-reduced beers. Dedicated gluten-free beers are brewed from grains that naturally lack gluten, such as sorghum, rice, or millet, making them safe for individuals with celiac disease. In contrast, gluten-reduced beers are produced using traditional grains but undergo an enzymatic process to reduce gluten levels, allowing those with mild gluten sensitivity to enjoy them. Both cater to the growing demand for gluten-free options among consumers.</p></p>
