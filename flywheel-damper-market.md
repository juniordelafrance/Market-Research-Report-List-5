<p><p>The Flywheel Damper market consists of various types based on the number of phases and design. Individual Bent Spring Flywheel Dampers are single-phase devices that mitigate torsional vibrations using a simple spring mechanism. One-phase Bent Spring Dampers enhance damping in one rotational direction, while Two-phase Bent Spring Dampers allow for effective vibration control in both directions. Three-phase Bent Spring Dampers provide advanced damping capabilities across multiple rotational frequencies, catering to high-performance applications by improving stability and reducing wear in mechanical systems.</p></p>
