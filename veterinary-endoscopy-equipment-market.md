<p><p>The veterinary endoscopy equipment market comprises several types, including flexible endoscopy, rigid endoscopy, capsule endoscopy, and others. Flexible endoscopes are used for non-invasive internal examinations, allowing for easier navigation in various animal anatomies. Rigid endoscopes provide a more stable view and are suitable for certain surgical procedures. Capsule endoscopy involves swallowing a small camera, capturing images of the gastrointestinal tract. Other types may include hybrid devices or specialized scopes for different veterinary applications, catering to specific diagnostic needs.</p></p>
