<p><p>Butyl rubber sealants are widely used for their excellent adhesion, flexibility, and resistance to moisture and chemicals. The market is divided into two main types: Vulcanized and Non-Vulcanized. Vulcanized butyl rubber sealants undergo a curing process, enhancing durability, heat resistance, and mechanical strength, making them ideal for high-performance applications like automotive and construction. Non-Vulcanized types remain uncured, offering easier application and reworkability, suitable for temporary seals or low-stress environments. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
