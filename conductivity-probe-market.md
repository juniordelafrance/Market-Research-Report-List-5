<p><p>The conductivity probe market comprises various types, including ring probes, pen probes, and needle probes, each designed for specific applications. Ring probes are often used for bulk solution measurements, providing a stable reading for larger samples. Pen probes offer portability and ease of use, making them ideal for field testing. Needle probes, known for their precision, are suitable for analyzing small volumes or specific locations within a sample.
