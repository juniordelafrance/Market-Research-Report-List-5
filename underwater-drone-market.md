<p><strong>The Underwater Drone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AUV</li><li>ROV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater drone market comprises two primary types: Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) and Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs). AUVs are self-propelled and operate independently using pre-programmed commands, making them ideal for tasks like environmental monitoring and mapping. In contrast, ROVs are tethered to a surface control unit, allowing real-time operator control. They are commonly used for underwater inspections, maintenance, and repairs. Both types serve diverse industries, including oil and gas, marine research, and underwater exploration.</p></p>
