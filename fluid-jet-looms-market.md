<p><ul><li> Water Jet Loom</li><li>Air Jet Loom </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluid-jet loom market comprises two primary types: water jet looms and air jet looms. Water jet looms utilize high-pressure water to propel the weft yarn through the warp, ideal for weaving fabrics like silk and synthetics. In contrast, air jet looms employ compressed air to insert the weft yarn, offering faster production speeds and versatility in handling lightweight materials. Both types are instrumental in modern textile manufacturing, catering to diverse fabric requirements and enhancing production efficiency.</p></p>
