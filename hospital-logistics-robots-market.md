<p><p>The hospital logistics robots market primarily consists of two types: Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) and mobile robots. AGVs are largely fixed-route vehicles that transport supplies and equipment along predetermined paths, ensuring efficient movement in a controlled environment. In contrast, mobile robots are more flexible and can navigate dynamically, adapting to their surroundings and transporting various items throughout the hospital. Both types enhance operational efficiency, reduce manual labor, and improve overall workflow within healthcare settings.</p></p>
