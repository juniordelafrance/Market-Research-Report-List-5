<p><ul><li>GDH-FAD</li><li>GOD</li><li>Electrochemistry</li><li>GDH</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The invasive blood glucose meter market encompasses various types of technology for measuring blood sugar levels. GDH-FAD (Glucose Dehydrogenase Flavin Adenine Dinucleotide) utilizes a specific enzyme for accurate readings. GOD (Glucose Oxidase) measures glucose by oxidizing it and detecting the resultant current. Electrochemistry involves measuring electric current produced during glucose oxidation. GDH (Glucose Dehydrogenase) is another enzymatic method, while "Other" includes alternative technologies or mixed methods that may not fit neatly into the first four categories.</p></p>
