<p><p>The bus turbocharger market features two primary types: mono turbo and twin turbo systems. Mono turbo systems utilize a single turbocharger to enhance engine performance, making them simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, twin turbo systems employ two turbochargers, allowing for improved efficiency and power across a broader range of engine speeds. This can lead to better acceleration and higher overall performance, catering to more demanding applications in modern bus designs. Each type serves distinct operational needs and market segments.</p></p>
