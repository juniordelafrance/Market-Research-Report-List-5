<p><ul><li>Lower Deck Container</li><li>Main Deck Container</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air container market is segmented into lower deck and main deck containers, each serving distinct roles in air cargo transportation. Lower deck containers are typically used in the belly holds of passenger aircraft, maximizing space for smaller cargo loads. In contrast, main deck containers are designed for dedicated freighters, allowing for larger and heavier shipments. Both types facilitate efficient loading and unloading, ensuring optimal use of aircraft capacity and supporting the growing demand for air freight services.</p></p>
