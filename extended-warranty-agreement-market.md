<p><p>The extended warranty agreement market typically consists of two main types: the Standard Protection Plan and the Accidental Protection Plan. The Standard Protection Plan covers mechanical failures and defects, providing repair or replacement for a specified duration beyond the manufacturer's warranty. In contrast, the Accidental Protection Plan extends coverage to damages resulting from accidents, spills, or drops, offering peace of mind for consumers concerned about unforeseen incidents. Both plans enhance consumer protection and extend the lifespan of purchased products.</p></p>
