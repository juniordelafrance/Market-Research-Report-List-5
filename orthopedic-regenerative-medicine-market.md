<p><p>The Orthopedic Regenerative Medicine market encompasses several innovative treatment types. Stem Cell Therapy involves utilizing either allogeneic (donor-derived) or autogeneic (patient-derived) bone cells, alongside bone grafting techniques to enhance healing. Plasma-based therapy, which optimizes healing via concentration of growth factors from the patient's blood, is also prominent. Additionally, tissue engineering using allografts integrates biological materials to repair or replace damaged tissues, supporting regeneration and improving patient outcomes in orthopedic conditions. Together, these therapies advance orthopedic care.</p></p>
