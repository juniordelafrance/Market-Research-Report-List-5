<p><p>A telecentric camera lens is a specialized optical component designed to maintain a constant magnification across varying object distances, thereby minimizing perspective distortion. This feature makes telecentric lenses particularly valuable in applications requiring precision measurements, such as industrial inspection, metrology, and semiconductor manufacturing. By ensuring that the lens’s optical axis remains perpendicular to the object surface, telecentric lenses provide high accuracy and reliability in imaging.</p><p>The Telecentric Camera Lens Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 3.80% during the forecast period. <p><p>The telecentric camera lens market features various types, including object square telephoto lenses and bi-telecentric lenses. Object square telephoto lenses maintain consistent magnification across the field of view, making them ideal for applications requiring precision imaging. Bi-telecentric lenses provide uniform magnification both vertically and horizontally, minimizing perspective distortion and enhancing measurement accuracy. These lenses are essential in fields such as industrial inspection, medical imaging, and scientific research, where image fidelity and dimensional accuracy are crucial.</p></p>
