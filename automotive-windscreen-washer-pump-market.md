<p><p>The automotive windscreen washer pump market includes two main types: Mono Pump and Dual Pump. Mono Pumps are single-unit systems designed to supply washer fluid to either the front or rear windshield, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Dual Pumps, on the other hand, feature two separate units or a single unit with dual outlets, enabling simultaneous cleaning of both front and rear windshields. Dual Pumps are more advanced, providing enhanced functionality and convenience, often found in modern vehicles with higher safety and comfort requirements.</p></p>
