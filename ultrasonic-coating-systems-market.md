<p><ul><li>Rotary-Type Ultrasonic Coating Systems</li><li>Flat-Type Ultrasonic Coating Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic coating systems market comprises primarily two types: rotary-type and flat-type systems. Rotary-type ultrasonic coating systems utilize a rotating mechanism to apply coatings evenly on cylindrical objects, ensuring consistent coverage. In contrast, flat-type ultrasonic coating systems are designed for flat surfaces, providing uniform application across larger areas. Both systems leverage ultrasonic vibrations to enhance coating adhesion and reduce material waste, making them essential in industries like electronics, automotive, and medical for precision and efficiency in surface treatment processes.</p></p>
