<p><p>The Diagnostic ECG Scanner market is divided into two main types: analogue and digital. Analogue ECG scanners utilize traditional technology to capture electrical signals from the heart, offering simplicity and ease of use, but may lack advanced features. In contrast, digital ECG scanners leverage modern technology for enhanced accuracy, ease of interpretation, and data storage. Digital models often provide real-time monitoring and connectivity features, making them more suitable for advanced diagnostic applications and remote patient monitoring in today’s healthcare environment.</p></p>
