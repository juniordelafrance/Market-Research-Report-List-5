<p><p>The stainless steel scrubber market comprises two main types: winding scrubbers and woven scrubbers. Winding scrubbers are made by coiling stainless steel wire, creating a dense and durable cleaning tool ideal for heavy-duty scrubbing. In contrast, woven scrubbers are crafted by interlacing strands of stainless steel, providing flexibility and a gentler cleaning finish, suitable for delicate surfaces. Both types effectively remove tough grime, rust, and deposits, making them popular for various household and industrial cleaning applications.</p></p>
