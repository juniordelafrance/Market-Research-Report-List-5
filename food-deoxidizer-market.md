<p><p>The food deoxidizer market encompasses two main types: metallic and non-metallic deoxidizers. Metallic deoxidizers, often containing iron or aluminum, work by removing oxygen from packaged food, thus prolonging shelf life and preventing spoilage. Non-metallic deoxidizers, typically comprised of natural substances like clay or activated carbon, absorb moisture and oxygen without using metals, offering a safer alternative for sensitive products. Both types play crucial roles in food preservation, ensuring freshness and maintaining quality during storage and transportation.</p></p>
