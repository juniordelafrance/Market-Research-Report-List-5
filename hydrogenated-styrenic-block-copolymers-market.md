<p><p>Hydrogenated styrenic block copolymers (HSBCs) primarily include Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene (SEBS) and Styrene-Ethylene-Propylene-Styrene (SEPS). SEBS is known for its superior weather resistance and mechanical properties, making it ideal for applications like adhesives, sealants, and flexible applications. SEPS, with enhanced elasticity and thermal stability, is often used in applications requiring improved performance in harsh environments. Both types are widely utilized in industries such as automotive, consumer goods, and medical products due to their versatility and durability.</p></p>
