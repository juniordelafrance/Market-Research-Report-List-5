<p><ul><li>Oriented Electrical Steel Strips</li><li>Non-oriented Electrical Steel Strips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical steel strips are specialized steel products used in electrical applications due to their magnetic properties. **Oriented Electrical Steel Strips** are designed with a specific grain orientation, offering superior magnetic properties in one direction, making them ideal for transformers and high-efficiency motors. **Non-oriented Electrical Steel Strips** have uniform magnetic properties in all directions, making them suitable for applications like rotors, stators, and small motors where multidirectional magnetization is required. Both types optimize energy efficiency and performance in electrical devices.</p></p>
