<p><strong>The Radiant Floor Heating System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Installation above Wooden Floor</li><li>Installation Under Subfloor</li><li>Ordinary Concrete Slab or Underground Installation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiant floor heating systems are categorized based on installation methods, highlighting their versatility. "Installation above wooden floors" places heating elements directly on the existing surface, ideal for retrofits. "Installation under subfloors" embeds heating components, providing efficient warmth without altering aesthetics. The "ordinary concrete slab" method incorporates heating within the concrete, ensuring uniform heat distribution for new builds. Lastly, "underground installation" utilizes buried heating pipes or cables, effectively warming spaces like basements while maximizing energy efficiency.</p></p>
