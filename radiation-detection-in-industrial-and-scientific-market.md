<p><p>Radiation detection in industrial and scientific markets involves various technologies, including Geiger counters, scintillation detectors, and solid-state detectors. Geiger counters are popular for their simplicity and effectiveness in measuring radiation levels. Scintillation detectors utilize scintillating materials to convert radiation into light, enhancing sensitivity. Solid-state detectors, using semiconductors, offer high resolution and efficiency for precise radiation measurement. Together, these devices cater to diverse applications such as environmental monitoring, nuclear safety, and medical diagnostics, ensuring accurate radiation analysis and safety compliance.</p></p>
