<p><p>The Medical Image Management market encompasses several key types including Medical Image Management Systems, which facilitate the storage, retrieval, and sharing of imaging data across healthcare facilities. Vendor Neutral Archives (VNAs) serve as centralized platforms for storing images from different systems, ensuring interoperability regardless of the originating vendor. Application-Independent Clinical Archives (AICA) provide a flexible, long-term solution for managing clinical data, enabling seamless access to images and associated medical information without reliance on specific applications.</p></p>
