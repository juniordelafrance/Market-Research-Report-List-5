<p><p>Static Light Scattering Nanoparticle Size Analyzers are categorized based on detection angles: Low Angle, Right Angle, and Multi-Angle. Low Angle analyzers focus on small scattering angles, providing high sensitivity for small particles and enabling size determination with minimal dilution. Right Angle analyzers operate at 90 degrees, offering a balance of resolution and sensitivity, suitable for a wide range of particle sizes. Multi-Angle analyzers capture data across multiple angles, enhancing the accuracy and size distribution analysis of complex nanoparticle samples.</p></p>
