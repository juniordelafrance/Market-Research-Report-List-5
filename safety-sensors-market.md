<p><p>The safety sensors market encompasses various types designed to enhance workplace safety. Safety light curtains create an invisible barrier that stops machinery when an object interrupts the beam. Safety laser scanners use laser technology to detect obstacles within a specified area, allowing for flexible safety zones. Single-beam safety sensors operate by emitting a single infrared beam that, when obstructed, triggers a safety response. Together, these technologies provide vital protection in industrial settings, reducing accident risks and ensuring compliance with safety regulations.</p></p>
