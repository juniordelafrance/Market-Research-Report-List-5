<p><p>The electronic medical thermometer market is segmented into various types based on measurement methods. Anal thermometers, known for their accuracy, are ideal for infants and medical settings. Ear thermometers use infrared technology for quick readings, making them convenient for home use. Forehead thermometers, also infrared-based, are easy to use and popular for quick checks. Oral thermometers provide reliable readings by measuring temperature sublingually, while axillary thermometers measure temperature under the arm, offering a non-invasive option but usually with less accuracy.</p></p>
