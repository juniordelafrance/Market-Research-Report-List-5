<p><ul><li>Full-fat Soy Flour</li><li>Defatted Soy Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soy flour is derived from ground soybeans and is categorized mainly into two types: full-fat soy flour and defatted soy flour. Full-fat soy flour retains the natural oil content of the soybeans, making it rich in nutrients and flavor, often used in baked goods and protein supplements. Defatted soy flour, on the other hand, has had most of its oil removed, resulting in a higher protein content and lower fat, commonly used for fortifying food products and in vegetarian diets.</p></p>
