<p><p>The Battery-Free RFID Sensor market is categorized into three types based on frequency: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra High Frequency (UHF). LF sensors operate between 125 kHz to 134 kHz, suitable for animal tracking and access control, offering long read range but slower data transfer. HF sensors, operating at 13.56 MHz, are ideal for applications like smart labels and payment systems, providing better data rates. UHF sensors, ranging from 300 MHz to 3 GHz, support longer read distances and are used in supply chain management and inventory tracking.</p></p>
