<p><p>The Fixed Surgical Suction Pump market is divided into two main types: pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic suction pumps operate using compressed air, offering portability and ease of use without reliance on electrical outlets, making them suitable for various surgical environments. In contrast, electric suction pumps provide consistent suction power, often featuring more advanced controls and improved efficiency, making them ideal for more complex surgeries. Both types play essential roles in maintaining a sterile environment by removing fluids during surgical procedures.</p></p>
