<p><p>The waterborne polyurethane adhesives market consists of three main types: anionic, cationic, and nonionic. Anionic types are characterized by their negative charge, enhancing adhesion to various substrates, particularly in textile applications. Cationic types, with their positive charge, offer improved bonding on surfaces with negative charges, making them suitable for coatings and sealants. Nonionic types, being neutral, provide versatility and compatibility with a range of materials while minimizing issues like foaming. Each type caters to different industrial needs based on their properties.</p></p>
