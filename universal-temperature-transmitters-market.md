<p><p>The Universal Temperature Transmitters market is segmented into two main types: Head Mount and DIN Rail Mount. Head Mount transmitters are designed for direct installation on sensors, providing a compact solution for temperature measurement in tight spaces. They are commonly used in industrial applications. On the other hand, DIN Rail Mount transmitters are designed for easy installation on standard DIN rails, allowing for flexibility in panel setups and facilitating easier wiring and maintenance in control systems.</p></p>
