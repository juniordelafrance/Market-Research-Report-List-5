<p><p>The Electronic Controlled Throttle Body market comprises three key components: the Actuator, Throttle Plate, and Throttle Position Sensor. The Actuator controls the throttle plate's movement, regulating airflow into the engine. The Throttle Plate, a mechanical valve, adjusts the air intake based on driver input and engine demands. The Throttle Position Sensor monitors the plate's angle, providing real-time feedback to the engine control unit for precise throttle management. Together, these components ensure efficient engine performance, fuel economy, and emissions control in modern vehicles.</p></p>
