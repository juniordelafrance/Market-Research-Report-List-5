<p><strong>The Smart Cash Registers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screen</li><li>Double Screen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart cash registers are categorized into single screen and double screen markets based on their display configurations. Single screen cash registers feature one display that serves both the customer and the cashier, streamlining transactions but limiting customer interaction. In contrast, double screen cash registers have two separate displays, one for the cashier and one facing the customer, enhancing engagement and transparency during transactions. This dual-screen setup fosters a better customer experience, making it increasingly popular in retail and hospitality sectors.</p></p>
